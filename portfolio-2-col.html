Top Flap

Protects the user from exposure to moving parts during the lifting action of the jack. Flap is lifted by the draw as it rises to the surface

Drawer Slot and Bracket

The bracket is mounted on a rod which is connected to a frame that is capable of moving laterally. This allows for it to slide horizontally into a complementary slot in the drawer. The design of the bracket was optimised to ensure an even distribution of the load of the drawer

Central Actuation Rod

The lifting bracket is immediately mounted onto this rod. This rod which extends vertically runs pallalel and is connected to a motorised lead screw to enable vertical motion of the bracket

User Interface

User selects their desired compartment to be lifted on the interface
