Lydia Edwards

Manufacturing and Documentation Lead

Lydia is responsible for the material purchases, parts ordering and making decisions on the manufacturing methods for the different parts of the system. As the set and props designer of Imperial Dramatic Society, she has a good deal of experience in the science and art of 'making'.

lydia.edwards17@ic.ac.uk

Jesidhn Pillai

Co-Team Manager & CAD Specialist

Having survived two gruelling year of his degree, Jesidhn is now a third-year student of MechanicaL Engineering at Imperial College London. In this project, he is responsible for the overall integration of the different CAD parts modelled by the different team members. He also oversees the progress of the project to ensure that it stays on schedule.

jp2617@ic.ac.uk

Advaith Sastry

Mechatronics Lead

As the mechatronics team lead, Advaith is responsible for optimising the selection of the motors, the development of the control system and the circuit diagram of our project. Spending his last summer as an intern for Cummins Inc., Advaith has gathered a good share of experience in the testing of electronic components, and working with microcontrollers.

advaith.sastry17@ic.ac.uk

Ossian Keith

Co-Team Manager and Programming Lead

As a committee member of DroneSoc and the co-founder of what was once #1 UK-based recreational drone review website, Ossian is hugely experienced in power electronics design. He is also capable of coding in several languages which would stand him in good stead in interfacing the software and hardware aspects of our project.

ossian.keith17@ic.ac.uk
